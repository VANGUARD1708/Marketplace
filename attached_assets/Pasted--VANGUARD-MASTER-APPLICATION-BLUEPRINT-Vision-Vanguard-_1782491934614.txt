# VANGUARD – MASTER APPLICATION BLUEPRINT

## Vision

Vanguard is an AI-powered trusted marketplace and business ecosystem where users can:

- Buy and sell products
- Offer and hire services
- Find jobs and recruit talent
- Build and manage companies
- Learn and teach through courses
- Invest in opportunities
- Use escrow-protected transactions
- Build trust and reputation
- Receive AI assistance and fraud protection
